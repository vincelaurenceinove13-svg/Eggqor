Our process

Crafted with
intention.

一

The Bread

Bread is toasted in butter in margarine for unmatched softness

二

The Egg

Eggs are soft-boiled for exactly 7 minutes, then cooled in an ice bath. Folded gently with Kewpie mayo — never over-mixed.

三

The Assembly

Crusts removed. Filling spread edge-to-edge. Cut diagonally with a single clean motion. Wrapped in washi paper and served immediately.

About us

Born from a
team's idea.

In 2025, Cassandra Cruda, Lyra De Castro, Nassleah Ismael, Vince Inovejas, and James Jordan and other Iqor students gather to brainstorm what kind sandwich will be selling and what brand name of the product. They came up with EggQor that is divided into two; "Egg", pertaining to the egg sandwich and "Qor" that means IQOR the section name.

They spent one day testing 15 bread recipes, sourcing eggs from market, and calibrating boiling times down to the second. EggQor opened in 2026 in a small corner of gymnasium court at Biñan Integrated National High School, no reservations, and a single item on the menu.

The bread was cut into half, forming a triangular shape. The stuffing for the bread includes the following that was later on mixed: boiled eggs, mayonnaise, and a pinch of salt & pepper. After the filling was stuffed with bread, it is now topped with parsley.

Today, we still make every sandwich toasted in a savory butter toasted. We still run out by noon. We still believe that the most meaningful things are simple ones, made with care.

一

Freshness Above All

Every ingredient arrives the morning it is used. Butter Bread baked at 7am. Eggs delivered by 6am. Doors open at 10am.

二

No Shortcuts, Ever

We peel every egg by hand. We never pre-make. When we run out, we close. Quality does not negotiate with convenience.

三

Rooted in Tradition

Our recipes honour the spirit of Japanese kissaten culture — the neighborhood café where simplicity and craftsmanship share a table.
